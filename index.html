Screen To Book

1.Take a smartphone/tablet

2.Take Frosted Glass (https://amzn.to/3wh4PIo)

3.Cut the Frosted Glass to the size of the screen

4.Peel and stick the Frosted Glass to the screen

5.Use a tape-roll to remove bubbles

6.Use some tape to secure the Frosted Glass and the device's screen to prevent bubbles and removal of the Frosted Glass

7.Done !
